RESEARCH & PROCESS

DESKRESEARCH

For my desk research, I searched for relevant and reliable sources, including articles, blogs, and case studies about technology in stadiums. This helped me gain a clearer understanding of the topic. I selected the most important and interesting findings to use in my project, such as social media and apps, QR codes and FastID, cashless payments, and in-seat delivery.

INTERVIEW

Besides my desk research, I also interviewed Cas van Mulken, a student who did an internship at Fortuna Sittard. I asked him questions to get more insight into the topic and hear his opinion about the use of technology in football stadiums. His experiences gave me a more practical view that added to what I found in my research.
